Meet the Founder

Sayali

At the heart of VerbiForge is our founder, Sayali, a dedicated Quality System Specialist at Sanofi with 6 years of extensive experience in translation services and multilingual project management. Her expertise spans pharmaceutical documentation, regulatory compliance, clinical trial materials, and cross-cultural communication strategies.

Throughout her career, Sayali has successfully managed numerous translation projects for Sanofi's global operations, ensuring seamless communication across 100+ languages for critical pharmaceutical documentation, patient information, and regulatory submissions. Her deep understanding of quality systems and compliance requirements has enabled her to develop rigorous translation protocols that maintain the highest standards of accuracy and regulatory compliance.

Under Sayali's leadership, VerbiForge has built a specialized network of certified translators with expertise in pharmaceutical, medical, and technical terminology. Her hands-on experience with Sanofi's diverse portfolio has shaped our quality-first approach, ensuring every translation meets the stringent standards required for healthcare and pharmaceutical communications.

Sayali's vision combines her pharmaceutical industry expertise with a passion for breaking down language barriers in healthcare. She understands that accurate translation in the medical field isn't just about words-it's about patient safety, regulatory compliance, and global accessibility to life-saving information.
