Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: September 29, 2025

At Verbiforge, accessible from www.verbiforge.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing information, and any other details you provide when registering or making a purchase.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send confirmations
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Send updates, promotional content, and important notifications
  • Ensure website security and prevent fraudulent activity

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties that help us operate our business (e.g., hosting providers, payment gateways, analytics).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing
  • Opt-out of promotional communications

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@verbiforge.com.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.

9. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@verbiforge.com
